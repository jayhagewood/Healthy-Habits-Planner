Easy Recipes for Two

1. Grilled Chicken Salad with Avocado & Spinach

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts
  • 4 cups spinach or mixed greens
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions: Grill chicken breasts, then mix spinach, avocado, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar in a bowl.

2. Baked Salmon with Quinoa and Steamed Veggies

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups steamed broccoli or cauliflower
  • Lemon wedges
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions: Bake salmon at 375Â°F for 15 minutes. Cook quinoa and steam vegetables. Drizzle with olive oil and serve with lemon wedges.

3. Stir-fried Shrimp with Bell Peppers & Broccoli

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 8 oz shrimp, peeled
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions: Stir-fry shrimp, bell peppers, and broccoli in olive oil until cooked through.
