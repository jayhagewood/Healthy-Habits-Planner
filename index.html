Easy Recipes for Two

1. Oatmeal with Berries

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups almond milk or water
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions: Cook oats with water or almond milk. Top with berries and chia seeds before serving.

2. Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions: Grill chicken, then combine greens and cucumber. Add olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
