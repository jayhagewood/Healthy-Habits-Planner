Easy Recipes for Two

1. Oatmeal with Berries (Breakfast)

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups water or almond milk
  • 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions: Cook oats with water or almond milk on the stovetop. Top with mixed berries and chia seeds before serving.

2. Grilled Chicken Salad (Lunch)

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 2 grilled chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions: Grill the chicken breasts. Combine greens and cucumber in a bowl. Slice chicken and add to the salad. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

3. Baked Salmon with Vegetables (Dinner)

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions: Bake salmon fillets at 375°F for 12-15 minutes. Steam the broccoli and cook quinoa according to package instructions. Drizzle salmon and broccoli with olive oil and lemon juice before serving.

4. Stir-fried Shrimp with Vegetables (Dinner)

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 8 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice

Instructions: Stir-fry shrimp, bell pepper, and onion in olive oil until cooked through. Serve over cauliflower rice.

5. Quinoa Salad (Lunch)

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions: Cook quinoa and let it cool. Mix with vegetables and toss in olive oil and lemon juice.
