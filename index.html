Bi-Weekly Shopping List

Weeks 1-2 Shopping List

  • Proteins: 6 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, 4 salmon fillets, 1 lb shrimp, 2 cod fillets, 1 lb ground turkey, 1 dozen eggs, 1 tub Greek yogurt, 1 pack turkey breast slices, Protein powder for shakes
  • Vegetables: 1 lb baby spinach, 1 lb broccoli florets, 1 lb mixed greens, 1 cucumber, 4 bell peppers, 1 lb asparagus, 1 red onion, 1 zucchini, 1 head cauliflower
  • Fruits: 1 pint blueberries, 1 pint raspberries, 4 apples, 4 bananas, 1 lemon
  • Grains & Carbs: Whole-wheat tortillas, whole-grain bread, 1 lb quinoa, 1 lb rolled oats, 1 bag brown rice, Cauliflower rice (optional)
  • Dairy/Alternatives: Almond milk (unsweetened), Low-fat cheddar cheese
  • Pantry/Staples: Olive oil, Balsamic vinegar, Almonds, Chia seeds, Flaxseeds, Peanut butter (no added sugar)
  • Spices/Seasoning: Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, cumin, onion powder

Weeks 3-4 Shopping List

  • Proteins: 6 boneless chicken thighs, 4 cod fillets, 1 lb shrimp, 1 lb lean ground beef, 1 dozen eggs, Greek yogurt, turkey breast slices, Protein powder for shakes
  • Vegetables: 1 lb baby spinach, 1 lb Brussels sprouts, 1 lb mixed greens, 1 cucumber, 1 lb cherry tomatoes, 4 bell peppers, 1 zucchini, 1 head cauliflower
  • Fruits: 1 pint strawberries, 1 pint blackberries, 4 apples, 4 bananas, 1 lemon
  • Grains & Carbs: Whole-wheat tortillas, whole-grain bread, quinoa, rolled oats, brown rice, Cauliflower rice
  • Dairy/Alternatives: Almond milk, Low-fat cheddar cheese
  • Pantry/Staples: Olive oil, Balsamic vinegar, Almonds, Chia seeds, Flaxseeds, Peanut butter (no added sugar)
  • Spices/Seasoning: Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, cumin, onion powder
